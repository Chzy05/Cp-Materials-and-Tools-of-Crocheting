Materials and Tools

Crocheting can require various types of tools and equipment that can improve the quality of our work. As a beginner we will only focus

on the most essential basic crochet materials which consist of the Yarn, Crochet hooks, Scissors, Tapestry needle, and Stitch Markers.


1. Yarn

This is the material we'll be crocheting with. This will depend on the person's project, it has

different colours and types such as wool or cotton that can add to create diverse textures to their

creations.




2. Crochet Hooks

This is the tool that we'll need to loop the yarn. This come's in various sizes and materials

like metal or wood which can add different textures and feeling in our hand while crocheting.




3. Scissors

This will be used to cut and lessen the risk of ruining the yarns' quality.




4. Tapestry Needle

Helps to weave the loose ends. A tapestry needle is a blunt needle with a large eye. The large

eye is useful to needleworkers because it can accommodate threads or fibres that are thicker than

ordinary sewing thread. Most tapestry needles are large enough to accommodate crochet thread

or embroidery floss, and many are large enough to accommodate yarn as well.




5. Stitch markers

Stitch markers can help to track your progress. It is a small tool that helps your track of

what round, row or stitch you're on in your crochet project.
